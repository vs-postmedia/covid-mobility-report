How the pandemic could change global transit

The COVID-19 pandemic caused transit ridership to plummet, throwing many operators into financial turmoil. A look at several of the world's largest transit systems show that transit ridership remains depressed, while car travel in several cities approaches or excedes pre-pandemic levels. Trips to the workplace, a major driver of both transportation modes, remains far below 2019 levels.
